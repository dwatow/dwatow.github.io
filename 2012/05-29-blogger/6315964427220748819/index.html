邁向成功的八個原則


PASSION
第一項就是「熱情」。
I'm driven by my passion.

Freeman Thomas
car designer, Daimler Chrysler




WORK
全靠努力
It's all hard work. Nothing comes easily. But I have a lot of fun.
Pupert Murdoch
big cheese CEO

GOOD
要成功，先得，然後自己成為高手（不斷練習）
To be successful put your nose down in something a get damn good at it.
Alex Garden
game developer

FOCUS
一切都跟能多專注在一件事上有關
I think it all has go with focusing yourself to one thing.
Norman Jewison
filmmarker

PUSH
對自己狠一點（自我突破）
Push yourself. physically, mentally, you gotta push, push, push.
David Gallo
marine scientist

SHYNESS
SELF-DOUBT
我向來懷疑自己，不夠好
I always had self-doubts.I wasn't good enough, wasn't smart enough. Ididn't think I'd make it.
Goldie Hawn
actor

It's not always to push yourself
要很下心來逼自己並不容易，因此，母親就派上用場啦....「我媽媽督促我」

SERVE
不能只服務自己，要服務更多人
It was a privilege to serve as a doctor.
Sherwin Nuland professor of surgery, Yale


IDEAS
有好的想法就是做一些簡單的事（下面那些字）
I had an idea-founding the frist micro-computer software company
Bill Gates
software guy

Listen
Observe
Be Curious
Ask Question
Problem Solve
Make Connections

聽
觀察
好奇
問問題
解決問題
進行連接


PERSIST
有毅力才會成功
Persistence is the number one reason for our success.
Joe Kraus
co-founder, Excite

即使失敗也要有毅力撐過去，要有毅力撐過爛事（CRAP）
Criticism批評
Rejection拒絕
Assholes爛人
Pressure壓力
